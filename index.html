WebSplit

WebSplit is a tiny full HTML5-JS speedrun timer that you can run in your browser. It'll allow to time your runs, create splits, measure your performance compared to previous runs in real time and more to come !

To begin creating a new timer, click on the New Timer button. If you already have a saved one, click on the Load Timer button.
